1. Mission Brief

Welcome to HIAD Factory

Hypersonic Inflatable Aerodynamic Decelerator (HIAD) is a deployable aeroshell technology. This wizard will guide you through generation and optimization of your shield for planetary entry.

Dual-Backend Solver Architecture

  • SPARTA DSMC (Local Docker): High-fidelity kinetic solver for rarefied hypersonic entry and chemical non-equilibrium.
  • PyFluent CFD (Remote SSH): Automated continuum CFD via Ansys Fluent for dense atmosphere survivability.
  • DeepXDE PINNs: Physics-Informed Neural Networks for flow field refinement and surrogate gap-filling.
  • MoP-SBO: Methodology of Physics-based Surrogate-Based Optimization for ultra-fast convergence.

Baseline: IRVE-3 / LOFTID

  • Diameter: 3.0m (Standard Flight Scale)
  • TPS Thickness: 0.0254m (1.0 inch)
  • Nose Radius: 0.191m (Hemispherical)
  • Mission: Earth Reentry / Mars Entry

"Inflatable technology allows for larger drag areas than rigid shields, enabling heavy payload delivery to Mars and high-energy Earth return."

Turbulence Modeling Backends: PyFluent (Continuum) Select the viscous model for continuum simulations.

• SST k-omega: Standard RANS model for turbulent boundary layers. (PyFluent Only)
• Laminar: Resolves viscous layers without turbulence modeling.

Note: SPARTA resolves viscosity kinetically at the molecular level; this setting will be ignored for DSMC runs.
⚠️ MODEL INCOMPATIBILITY: SPARTA (DSMC) resolves viscosity kinetically via VSS (Variable Soft Sphere). Viscous/Turbulent models are for continuum backends only.
* SST k-omega applies to PyFluent; SPARTA uses kinetic VSS.

• Mars (6-Species): CO2, N2, CO, O, C, N. Carbon-dioxide dissociation baseline.
