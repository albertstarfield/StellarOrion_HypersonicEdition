1. Mission Brief

Welcome to HIAD Factory

Hypersonic Inflatable Aerodynamic Decelerator (HIAD) is a deployable aeroshell technology. This wizard will guide you through generation and optimization of your shield for planetary entry.

What is this?

Automated design and multi-fidelity simulation pipeline for inflatable hypersonic shields. Supports high-fidelity Earth (NRLMSIS 2.1) and Mars (MSL-grade) atmospheric modeling. Uses SPARTA for DSMC analysis and PyTorch for Metamodel optimization.

Adjustment Slider Value
Error Tol ?
Error Tolerance The allowed variance between the target and the achieved value. Smaller values force tighter optimization convergence.
Ballistic Coeff (β) ?
Ballistic Coefficient (β) Unit: kg/m² Measures an object's ability to overcome air resistance. Lower β results in higher altitude deceleration.
Stagnation Heat ?
Stagnation Heat Unit: W/cm² or MW/m² Peak convective heat flux at the nose stagnation point. Primary target for TPS design.
Peak g-load ?
Peak g-load Unit: g Maximum deceleration force experienced during entry, measured in Earth gravities.
Backface Temp (C) ?
Backface Temp Unit: Celsius (°C) Target temperature for the inner surface of the skin. Must protect the payload.
